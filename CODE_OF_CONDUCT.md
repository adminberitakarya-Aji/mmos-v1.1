# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors, maintainers, and community members, pledge to make participation in the MMOS community a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

We are committed to fostering an open, collaborative, and inclusive environment regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

# Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and considerate.
* Giving and accepting constructive feedback.
* Focusing discussions on technical merit.
* Helping new contributors learn the project.
* Being open to different viewpoints.
* Taking responsibility for mistakes.
* Acting professionally in public discussions.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
* Personal attacks or insults.
* Trolling or inflammatory comments.
* Publishing private information without permission.
* Deliberate disruption of discussions.
* Any behavior that creates an unsafe or hostile environment.

---

# Our Community Values

The MMOS community values:

* Respect
* Collaboration
* Transparency
* Professionalism
* Technical Excellence
* Long-Term Thinking

Disagreements are expected, but they should always remain respectful and focused on improving the project.

---

# Architectural Discussions

MMOS encourages constructive technical debate.

Contributors are welcome to challenge ideas, designs, or implementations.

However:

* Critique ideas, not people.
* Support proposals with technical reasoning.
* Respect previous architectural decisions.
* Follow the ADR process for architectural changes.

Healthy technical discussion strengthens the project.

---

# Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate these guidelines.

---

# Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* GitHub repositories
* Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Documentation
* Project communication channels
* Community events related to MMOS

---

# Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers through the project's official communication channels.

Reports will be reviewed fairly and handled with appropriate confidentiality whenever possible.

---

# Enforcement

Maintainers may take actions including:

* Private warning
* Request for corrective action
* Temporary suspension from participation
* Permanent removal from the community for severe or repeated violations

Enforcement decisions are made to protect the health and sustainability of the MMOS community.
