**Jakarta, Indonesia — January 15, 2026**

The government today announced a nationwide digital transformation initiative aimed at accelerating modernization across public services and empowering small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) through digital technology.

## Key Highlights

- Five-year implementation plan
- More than 10,000 government offices involved
- Digital adoption support for SMEs
- National cybersecurity improvements
- Public service modernization
- Increased investment in digital infrastructure

## Background

Digital transformation has become a strategic priority for governments worldwide as public expectations for efficient digital services continue to increase.

The initiative aligns with broader national efforts to accelerate innovation, economic competitiveness, and technology adoption.

## Conclusion

The national digital transformation initiative represents one of the largest modernization programs undertaken by the government. If successfully implemented, it is expected to improve public services, strengthen the digital economy, and increase technology adoption across both government institutions and businesses.
